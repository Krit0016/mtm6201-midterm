Our Story

Happiness within reach of ice cream.

How it all started

Happy Penguin is an ice cream company that has been delighting customers with its unique flavors for over four decades. Our ice cream is made with the freshest fruits and high-quality ingredients like peanuts and almonds.

Starting as a small ice cream truck in 1980, we have grown to have a presence in numerous locations across Canada, including supermarkets and convenience stores. Despite our growth, we remain committed to creating high-quality, delicious ice cream that brings joy to our customers' lives.

Made with Love

Get ready to indulge in a sweet treat that's made with love at Happy Penguin Ice Cream Shop! We take great pride in creating each and every scoop with only the highest-quality ingredients and traditional techniques, resulting in unique and exciting flavor combinations that are sure to tantalize your taste buds.

From classic favorites to more adventurous options like lavender honey and blackberry ginger, our ice cream is made in small batches to ensure maximum freshness. Plus, our friendly staff is passionate about sharing their love for ice cream and creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere that will have you coming back for more.

At Happy Penguin Ice Cream Shop, we believe that ice cream is more than just a dessert - it's an experience that brings people together, creates memories, and spreads happiness. So come on in and try our made-with-love ice cream for yourself. We guarantee you'll be hooked from the first scoop!
